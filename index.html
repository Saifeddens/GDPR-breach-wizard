GDPR Data Breach Notification Wizard

A simple web-based proof of concept that helps organizations assess whether a personal data breach may require notification under GDPR Articles 33 and 34.

Project Purpose

  • Guide users through a breach assessment process
  • Translate GDPR rules into a simple workflow
  • Support decision-making for breach notification
  • Generate a draft notification in later steps
